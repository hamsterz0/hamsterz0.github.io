Konnect Me

Best use of Cisco API

Konnect Me is an Android Applicaton that creates an event and sends an Interactive Voice Response invite to all the users in the event group. It then records the recievers response and gives a detailed report of who all accepted the invitaion and who didn't

Nims Game

Best CSE 3330 Project

Nims is an Android Game which gives the user the choice to play the game with either an AI or another user. The AI has 3 dificulties; easy, intermediate and hard. The game also keeps track of the highscore and the highscore is calculated based on the time the user takes to beat the AI

ROVER

Created a ROVER that can dig and collect stone samples, has GPS tracking and drive in rough terrains.
